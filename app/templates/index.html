Thank you for you interest in Brave's BPC Program. This program is intended to be used by members of Brave Collective to help them build whatever Brave needs.

The table below contains all of the available BPCs the program has. Please use the search box to filter the list if there is something specific you are looking for.

The list is broken down as follows:

  • Items in yellow have been donated to the program.
  • Items in green have a BPO owned by the program.
  • Items with ME/TE in blue are max researched
  • Items with '[+]' have multiple variations available. Hint: Sub-optimal variations are great for invention.

Additionally, please keep the following rules in mind when making requests:

  • Requests are limited to 10 BPCs at a time. Requests for more than 10 BPCs at a time are not allowed to be submitted.
  • The form will not let you request more than we have in stock. However, just because you make a request does not mean it will be completely filled. Exactly what to fill is at the discretion of the program members
  • You are limited to 1 request at a time. This is per person not account.
  • The program operates out of Dunk's Laboratory in GE-8JV. All requests will be filled there.

If you would like to make donations to be BPC program, please submit contracts to 'Brave Industries' corporation.
